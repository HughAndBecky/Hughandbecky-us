Woodworking

The scale and utility of my projects have a great range. Pragmatic need generally drives my wood working projects. Designers, talk about intentionality and how an object expresses some essence, but in wood working, I find the design and engineering needs converge. While I am working on a project in the building phase, I am motivate by I how I think people are going to love to touch and look at the finished product.
