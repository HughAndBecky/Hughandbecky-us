Formation of verbs in u̱t‑Maꞌin

Rebecca Paterson
Project Handout

Abstract

u̱t‑Maꞌin verbs occur with suffixes that display one set of tense/direction distinctions. Other TAM designations are coded by auxiliaries. This is a first attempt at describing the range of meanings encoded on verbs for the working group session of faculty and graduate students at the University of Oregon.
