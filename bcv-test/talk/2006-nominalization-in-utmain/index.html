Nominalization and Auxiliaries in u̱t‑Maꞌin

Rebecca Paterson
Master Class
Project Slides Handout

Abstract

Having worked with two speakers of the u̱t‑MaꞌRor variety of u̱t‑Maꞌin, I analyzed the use of nominalized verb forms in some auxiliary constructions. This presentation was of very preliminary analysis of data collected during the seminar consulting sessions.
