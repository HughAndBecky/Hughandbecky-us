Additional OLAC Collections

Hugh Paterson III
15 Jun, 2024 3 min read Jot, Research Note
Project

In 2022 I published about the lack of DCMIType usage to indicate collection records within OLAC records. That publication didn’t look at the use of <dc:type></dc:type> in an unqualified way to indicate collections. The analysis of that original paper should be revisited, or amended in this light. Today I found a new-to-me and interesting method to discovering in a qantifiable way additional un-marked collection reocords. It seems that the OLAC provider from cocoon crdo.vjf.cnrs.fr as not used the DCMIType declaration for collections but has used an undeclared type of <dc:type>archives</dc:type>. Archives are sets of artifacts and therefore meet the DCMIType definiition for collection. A quick search of the OLAC dataset grep -i '<dc:type>archives</dc:type>' ListRecords-20210718.xml | uniq -c indicates that there are 314 of these records. There were no records indicated for the terms set, collection, or group. However, there were quite a few with the dc:type term corpus
