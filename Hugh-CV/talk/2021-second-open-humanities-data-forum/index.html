Introduction to the JOHD special collection “Language Documentation: Assessment and Recognition”

Hugh Paterson III, Richard Griscom, Lauren B. Collister
Conference
Project Project Slides Watch YouTube - Richard and Hugh YouTube - Lauren

Abstract

In this presentation, split across to video segments the editors of the JOHD special collection “Language Documentation: Assessment and Recognition” present some of the thought behind the need for and the editorial intent of the special collection.
